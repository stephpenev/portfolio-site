ICSC 2018-19 | Exhibit, Print + Digital Design

I led the art direction and branding of the new company exhibit for the 2018 and 2019 ICSC Retail Conference held in Toronto at the Metro Toronto Convention Centre. I coordinated the design and production of the booth and a new retail leasing app; developed all print and video graphics, and managed all logistics of First Gulf’s participation at the show, from 2015 to 2019.

The annual event is the most visible marketing initiative that First Gulf is involved in. The goal of the 2018 redesign was to evoke openness, transparency and a cohesive, elegant physicality.
